Who We Are

Singapore's Most Trusted Events Partner

Compass Events is a Singapore-based, full-service event management company specialising in conferences, corporate events, hybrid experiences, and international productions. We combine strategic thinking with flawless execution to create events that move people and drive results.

Our team of seasoned producers, creative directors, and logistics experts has delivered events for global financial institutions, healthcare leaders, technology companies, and government agencies — always on brief, on budget, and beyond expectations.

Our Expertise

Five Verticals. One Standard.

Conference Management

Large-scale international and regional conferences, plenary sessions, breakouts, and gala dinners. We manage speakers, AV, registration, and every logistical detail so you can focus on content.

  • ✦ Venue sourcing & contracting
  • ✦ Speaker management & AV production
  • ✦ Delegate registration & comms
  • ✦ On-site coordination

Corporate Events

From intimate leadership dinners to company-wide town halls, we bring your brand and culture to life. Each event is tailored to your objectives, values, and audience.

  • ✦ Gala dinners & award ceremonies
  • ✦ Leadership & incentive retreats
  • ✦ Product launches & roadshows
  • ✦ Team building programmes

Hybrid Events

Seamlessly connect physical and virtual audiences through broadcast-quality production, interactive digital platforms, and real-time engagement tools.

  • ✦ Live-streaming & broadcast production
  • ✦ Virtual platform setup & support
  • ✦ Audience Q&A & polling
  • ✦ Post-event content editing

Overseas Events

Taking your event beyond Singapore? Our regional network spans Bangkok, Hong Kong, Jakarta, Tokyo, and more. We handle cross-border logistics so nothing gets lost in translation.

  • ✦ Pan-Asia venue sourcing
  • ✦ Local supplier management
  • ✦ Travel & accommodation logistics
  • ✦ Cultural & regulatory guidance

Exhibition Design & Build

Stunning booths and experiential spaces that stop attendees in their tracks. Our in-house design team crafts custom structures that express your brand with impact.

  • ✦ Custom booth design & 3D renders
  • ✦ Fabrication & installation
  • ✦ Interactive digital displays
  • ✦ Post-event dismantling & storage
